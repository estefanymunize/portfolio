Redesigning Conversational AI Onboarding

Users were abandoning an AI assistant in the first session — not because the AI was bad, but because the onboarding set the wrong expectations. Through diary studies and contextual interviews I identified the trust collapse moment, then redesigned the first-five-minutes experience to anchor confidence before complexity. Drop-off fell 34%.

AI Trust & Transparency Dashboard

A B2B platform needed to satisfy new EU AI Act transparency requirements without turning every screen into a legal disclaimer. I designed a "confidence layer" — a persistent but non-intrusive panel that explains AI reasoning in plain language, giving users the context they need without interrupting their workflow.

Voice UI for an AI Health Assistant

Designing voice interaction patterns for a health companion app where users are often anxious or unwell. I created a multimodal system that gracefully degrades from voice to touch, uses measured pacing in responses, and never sounds cheerful when someone is in pain. Satisfaction scores rose 28 points in testing.

AI Content Moderation Interface

Human moderators using AI-assisted tools were experiencing high decision fatigue and over-relying on model outputs. I redesigned the review queue to surface model confidence alongside dissenting signals, giving reviewers the information to exercise genuine judgement rather than rubber-stamp AI decisions.
