About me

Designing with
people at the centre

I'm a UX designer who believes that every frustrating interface is a solvable problem. My work lives in the gap between what a product does and what people actually need it to do.

Currently based in London, open to freelance and full-time opportunities in product design, UX research, and digital experience.
