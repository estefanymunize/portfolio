Quick answers

Common questions

  • Are you available for freelance work?

    Yes — I'm currently accepting freelance and contract projects, especially in AI, fintech, and health tech. Remote-first, open to occasional on-site in London.

  • What's your typical project engagement?

    It varies — I can join for a focused sprint (2–4 weeks) to audit and fix a specific experience, or embed longer-term on a product team for research, design, and testing cycles.

  • Do you work with early-stage startups?

    Absolutely. Some of my most interesting work has been at the 0→1 stage, helping founders understand their users before committing to a product direction.

  • Can you help with accessibility audits?

    Yes — accessibility review and remediation is a standard part of how I work, not an add-on. I audit against WCAG 2.1 AA as a baseline.
